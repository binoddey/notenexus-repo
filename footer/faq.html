Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about using Note Nexus.

Is Note Nexus completely free?
Yes! Note Nexus is a 100% free initiative built by students, for students. All notes, roadmaps, and community access are free of charge.
Are the notes verified?
We try our best. The notes are curated from high-performing students and vetted by our internal team. However, we always recommend cross-referencing with your standard textbooks for 100% accuracy.
Can I contribute my own notes?
Absolutely! We love contributors. You can join our Discord server or use the "Contact Us" page to submit your notes. Contributors get a special badge on our community page.
How do I download the notes?
Go to the Notes Library, select your subject/year filter, and click the "View Notes" button. This will take you to a Google Drive link where you can view or download the PDF.
Which University syllabus do you follow?
Currently, our material is optimized for generalised University B.Tech curriculum, but since engineering concepts are universal, students from all universities and institutes find them equally useful.
